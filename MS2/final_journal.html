Domain/Topic:

Counterfeit identities in the Cyberspace

Counterfeit identities:

• like/hate/comment bots
• internet trolls
• and what else...

Research Questions:

* What count as cyber counterfeit identities?
* What forms do they present themselves as and what activities do they create?
* How are counterfeit identities affect our social interaction in online communities?
* How are counterfeit identities affecting social issues and changing social dialogues?
* What are the impact they have on our overall cyberspace environment?
* How are they generated?
* Who generates counterfeit identities?

Concept Statement

I want to study about counterfeit identities in the modern cyberspace

because I want to find out why such a phenomenon is so pervasive, who manufactured these identities, who benefit from them and what impact these identities have on our overall social issues, online dialogues and personal interactions,

in order to inform the public about the scale and the influence such a phenomenon have on us, to invite the public to join the discussion about our current net environment.
