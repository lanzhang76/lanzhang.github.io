Facts:

* Scientific Name: Asteroidea
* Echinoderms/Phylum Echinodermata
* Carnivores/Marine invertebrates
* Live Up to 35 years
* 4.7 to 9.4 inch
* An echinoderm has no obvious left or right body, but top or bottom side
* Not fish but echinoderm, closer to sea urchin or sand dollars
* They have tube feet or podia (Oceanicresearc.org)
* expand and extract the tube feet, they walk
* “Benthic” – Living at the bottom
* bilateral - Two sides
* bumps on the body surface are called “Dermal Branchiae” - used to absorb oxygen
* pedicellaria on the skin are used to pluck things off
* Madreporite - single colored spot–used to filter water between the water vascular and the ocean
* The sea star has a light-sensitive organs at the tip of each ray, called an eye spot. It detects the presence and direction of light, and the seem to have idea where it is headed
* Starfish has no brains or blood, but its nervous system is spread through its arms
* Starfish have one eye on each tip of the arm

Starfish' main cool features:

* Limbs regeneration
* Starfish’s “Eyes” and nervous system are spread on the arms(no brain or blood), their entire arms are a connected sensor
* When feeding, starfish evert their stomach out through their mouth and it goes back after digestion
